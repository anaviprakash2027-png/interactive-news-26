Going big because we all go home at some point.


My name is Anavi Prakash, and I am a junior at Northwestern University studying journalism and social policy with a minor in data science. I primarily have experience in print and audio reporting, but love everything that brings text and multimedia elements together.

In all my reporting, I like to think big and engage the community in the stories I’m telling, whether during the reporting process or when looking at the final product. If we aren’t reporting for the community, then what’s the point?

You can reach me at anaviprakash2027@u.northwestern.edu. I look forward to hearing from you!
